BingoSprout

Plant numbers, shape the board, and turn exact sums into satisfying Bingo clears.

A Fast Number Strategy Puzzle

BingoSprout combines a growing number board with quick tactical choices. Choose from a 3x3 number pool, plant tiles into open cells, and swap adjacent numbers to match the target sums shown for each row and column.

Clear horizontal and vertical sequences, build combos, earn stars, and keep your board under control before time runs out.

Game Features

  • Plant and plan.
    Place numbers where they can complete exact target sums.
  • Swap with purpose.
    Move adjacent tiles only when the swap creates a Bingo.
  • Clear every direction.
    Find horizontal, vertical, and cross-Bingo combinations.
  • Grow through 50 levels.
    Chase better scores, more stars, and stronger combos.

Designed for iPhone and iPad

BingoSprout supports iOS 13 and later. Core gameplay and local progress remain available offline.
